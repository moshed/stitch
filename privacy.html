Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Stitch (“the app”) is built to respect your privacy. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

We do not collect your data

Stitch does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking. We have no servers that receive your data.

Your photos stay on your device

Stitch needs access to your photo library so it can find your screenshots and, if you choose, save the combined image back. All image processing happens entirely on your device. Your screenshots and stitched images are never uploaded or sent anywhere.

Purchases

Stitch Pro (a one-time purchase or an auto-renewing subscription) is handled by Apple through the App Store. Apple processes the transaction; Stitch does not receive or store your name, payment details, or Apple Account information. Subscriptions are managed in your device’s Settings.

Children

Stitch does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
