How do I stitch screenshots?

Take several screenshots of a scrolling page. Open Stitch → the Photos tab, tap the screenshots in order (or use a Suggested group), then tap Stitch. You can also share screenshots to Stitch straight from the iOS share sheet.

Stitch isn’t in my share sheet

Share any screenshot, scroll the app-icon row to the right, tap More, and enable Stitch. If it’s missing right after installing, open the app once and, if needed, restart your phone so iOS registers the extension.

What does Stitch Pro include?

Pro removes the free limit (one stitch every 3 days) and the small watermark — giving you unlimited stitches with no watermark. It’s available as a one-time Lifetime purchase or a monthly subscription with a free trial.

I already bought Pro — how do I restore it?

Open Settings in the app and tap Restore Purchases.

How do I cancel a subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple: open the iOS Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions.
